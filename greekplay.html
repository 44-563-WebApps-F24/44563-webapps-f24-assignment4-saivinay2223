Sai Vinay Kumar Bikki presents a Greek play by Sophocles

Philoctetes by Sophocles

Neoptolemus:

But what would you have me do?

Philoctetes:

Swear to me, child, by the mighty Zeus, lord of oaths.

Neoptolemus:

What is the oath you would have me swear?

Philoctetes:

Swear that you will convey me to my home, not to Troy.

Neoptolemus:

I swear it, by Zeus, as you ask.

Philoctetes:

And now, my son, give me your hand as a pledge.

Neoptolemus:

Here, take it. I shall not fail you.
